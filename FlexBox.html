Display

Configuration and Battery

Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra is loaded with a robust Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 chipset and an Adreno 730 GPU unit.

Storage and Connectivity

Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra is equipped with 256GB of non-expandable internal storage.
